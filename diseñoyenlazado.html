Design and Dink

Design: They are errors in the source code. They can be due to misspelled reserved words, erroneous or incomplete expressions, variables that have not been declared, etc





Dink: They usually refer to functions that are not defined in any of the object files or libraries. We may have forgotten to include some library, or some object file, or we may have forgotten to define some function or variable, or we have done it wrong
